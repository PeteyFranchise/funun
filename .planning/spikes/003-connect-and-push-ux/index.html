Step 1

Connect Buffer

Buffer doesn't offer one-click connect for new apps yet. Instead, paste a personal API key: in Buffer go to Settings → API, create a key, and paste it below. Funūn stores it encrypted and only uses it to schedule the posts you approve.

Step 2

Map your platforms to Buffer channels

We found these channels on your Buffer account. Confirm which Buffer channel each Funūn platform posts to.

YouTube Shorts isn't connected on your Buffer plan, so those 1 slot(s) will be skipped. Connect it in Buffer, or export those separately.

Step 3

Push & track status

Your 4-week calendar. Push schedules every mapped slot into Buffer's queue; skipped slots stay in Funūn only.
