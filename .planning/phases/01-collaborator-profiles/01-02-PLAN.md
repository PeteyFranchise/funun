<objective>
Wire the CollaboratorPicker (built in Plan 01) into MetadataStudio's composer rows so an artist auto-fills writer rights data from the global roster without re-entry, and surface the missing-IPI condition both on the row and in the project readiness breakdown. This is the COLLAB-03 vertical slice for the metadata/track path.

Purpose: COLLAB-03 (select collaborators when working in a track, auto-fill contact + rights data) for the MetadataStudio composer flow; D-01 (augment, don't replace), D-02 (auto-fill name/PRO/IPI/email/phone only), D-03 (flag missing IPI), D-05 (warning surfaces on readiness checklist), D-06 (inline add new), D-07 (save-to-profile nudge).

Output: An augmented ComposerEditor with a per-row picker, an inline missing-IPI chip, a save-to-profile nudge on rights-identity fields, and a readiness sub-check that downgrades the metadata item to 'warning' when a roster-picked composer is missing an IPI.
</objective>
