# Plan 12-10: Admin Featured & Sponsored Placements

<objective>
Add admin-managed featured/sponsored placement tooling and feed rendering support for members, projects, opportunities, partner cards, curated programs, and future paid placements.
</objective>

<safety_boundary>
This plan is an admin-curated placement slice, not self-serve advertising. It may support labels and future monetization fields, but it must not ship billing, targeting, budgets, ad analytics, or advertiser-facing campaign creation.
</safety_boundary>

<tasks>

<task type="auto">
  <name>Task 1: Add placement service and admin API</name>
  <acceptance_criteria>
    - Only admin-capable users can create/update placements.
    - Placements have type, label, active window, destination, priority, and safety status.
    - Inactive or expired placements never appear in the feed.
    - Hidden/removed placements never appear in the feed.
    - Destination objects are public/visible before activation.
  </acceptance_criteria>
</task>

<task type="auto">
  <name>Task 2: Add admin management UI</name>
  <acceptance_criteria>
    - Admins can create, schedule, pause, and archive placements.
    - Preview makes labels and destination clear.
    - No self-serve ad buying, billing, ad analytics, or targeting workflow appears.
    - Sponsored, Featured, Partner, Program, and Opportunity labels are visually explicit.
  </acceptance_criteria>
</task>

<task type="auto">
  <name>Task 3: Verify feed rendering labels</name>
  <acceptance_criteria>
    - Feed cards clearly distinguish featured, sponsored, partner, program, and opportunity placements.
    - Placements do not masquerade as organic user posts.
  </acceptance_criteria>
</task>

</tasks>
