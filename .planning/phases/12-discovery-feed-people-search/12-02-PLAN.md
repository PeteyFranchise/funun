# Plan 12-02: Feed Schema, RLS & Indexes

<objective>
Create the database foundation for Green Room posts, comments, reactions, reposts, curated placements, and custom audience storage.
</objective>

<tasks>

<task type="auto">
  <name>Task 1: Design feed tables</name>
  <acceptance_criteria>
    - Feed posts store author, type, body, visibility, status, linked object, allow_resharing, timestamps, and moderation fields.
    - Comments, reactions, reposts, and curated placements have independent lifecycle fields.
    - Custom audience data is queryable and bounded.
  </acceptance_criteria>
</task>

<task type="auto">
  <name>Task 2: Add RLS policies</name>
  <acceptance_criteria>
    - Owners manage their own posts and comments.
    - Feed reads exclude blocked relationships in both directions.
    - Drafts are owner-only.
    - Custom audience reads are enforceable without client trust.
  </acceptance_criteria>
</task>

<task type="auto">
  <name>Task 3: Add indexes and constraints</name>
  <acceptance_criteria>
    - Cursor pagination by published_at/id is indexed.
    - Tab queries can filter by author graph, visibility, post type, and placement window.
    - Reactions are unique per user/post/reaction type where appropriate.
  </acceptance_criteria>
</task>

</tasks>
