# Plan 12-05: Comments & Reactions

<objective>
Add first-party comments and reactions for Green Room posts while keeping the interaction layer privacy-aware and easy to moderate.
</objective>

<tasks>

<task type="auto">
  <name>Task 1: Add comments API</name>
  <acceptance_criteria>
    - Users can comment only on posts they can see.
    - Users can delete their own comments.
    - Post owners can hide/remove comments from their own posts.
  </acceptance_criteria>
</task>

<task type="auto">
  <name>Task 2: Add reactions API</name>
  <acceptance_criteria>
    - Supports Like, Love, Fire, Congrats, Inspired, Helpful, and Interested.
    - Users can add/remove their own reactions only.
    - Reaction totals do not leak hidden or blocked posts.
  </acceptance_criteria>
</task>

<task type="auto">
  <name>Task 3: Add UI primitives</name>
  <acceptance_criteria>
    - Feed cards show counts and the viewer's selected reaction.
    - Comment composer handles optimistic UI without trusting client state for final counts.
  </acceptance_criteria>
</task>

</tasks>
