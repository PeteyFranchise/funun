## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-10-17 | Tampering | note text from the composer | medium | mitigate | Composer caps at maxLength=200 client-side; the Plan-03 route's `buildConnectRequest()` re-validates and the Postgres CHECK is the hard backstop — client cap is convenience only, not the security boundary |
| T-10-18 | Tampering | forging connectionId / addressee to accept a request not addressed to the viewer | high | mitigate | The PATCH is gated by the Plan-03 route's session client + migration 035's `connections_update_addressee` RLS (only the addressee may accept/decline) — the button cannot bypass it; a forged connectionId simply fails the RLS UPDATE |
| T-10-19 | Information Disclosure | connect-state query leaking a connection the viewer is not part of | low | mitigate | The pair query relies on `connections_select_participant` RLS (`requester_id = auth.uid() OR addressee_id = auth.uid()`); a non-participant gets no row regardless of the client-supplied `.or()` filter |
