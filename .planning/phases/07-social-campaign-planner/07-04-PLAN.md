<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| client → generate/export API | Authenticated artist requests; /api excluded from middleware, each route is its own auth boundary |
| campaignId/slotId params → campaign row | Caller-supplied ids could target another user's campaign (IDOR) |
| slot caption free text → CSV cell | Artist-authored caption text flows into a downloadable CSV |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-07-12 | Elevation of Privilege (IDOR) | generate + export routes | high | mitigate | Both routes load the campaign scoped by `.eq('user_id', user.id)` and 404 before reading posts — a cross-user campaignId/slotId cannot generate or export another user's data |
| T-07-13 | Spoofing | missing per-route auth | high | mitigate | Both handlers independently call `auth.getUser()` and 401 — /api is excluded from middleware (RESEARCH.md V2) |
| T-07-14 | Tampering (CSV structure injection) | export csvCell | low | mitigate | Reuse lib/metadata/export.ts's `csvCell` quote/comma/newline escaping verbatim — captions with commas/quotes/newlines cannot break CSV row structure |
| T-07-15 | Tampering (spreadsheet formula injection) | export caption cells | low | accept | csvCell does not neutralize leading `=`/`+`/`@` formula triggers; captions are artist-authored (self-harm only) and the CSV targets Buffer (not Excel) — RESEARCH.md Security Domain accepts this; no extra mitigation |
| T-07-16 | Information Disclosure | export cover_art_url in CSV | low | accept | Image URL is the project's own already-signed cover_art_url, exported by the owning artist for their own Buffer account — no cross-tenant exposure |
</threat_model>
