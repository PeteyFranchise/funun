# 16-01: Buyer Identity, Capability & Verification Model

## Objective

Plan the sync-buyer identity layer before any portal code ships. Decide how buyer accounts relate to the Phase 15 capability model, what verification states exist, and what buyer permissions are safe before and after verification.

## Scope

- Research existing `capability_grants`, `artist_profiles`, admin invite, and auth patterns.
- Decide whether to add `buyer` as a new capability, create a separate `buyer_profiles` table, or use both.
- Define buyer verification states and permission tiers.
- Define buyer invitation/claim flow for Hook-style early buyers.
- Document what buyer accounts can and cannot do in Green Room, DMs, People Search, and public profiles.

## Acceptance Criteria

- A concrete buyer account model is chosen and documented.
- Verification states are named and mapped to allowed actions.
- Admin invite/approval flow is specified.
- Existing artist/industry accounts can or cannot add buyer capability by explicit decision.
- Security notes cover spoofed buyer accounts, spam, scraping, and unauthorized profile access.

## Validation

- Review against Phase 13 Trust & Safety plans.
- Review against Phase 15 capability-grants implementation.
- Confirm no buyer role grants broad social privileges by default.
