Establish the fully-separate buyer identity layer: buyer orgs, org members with two permission tiers, org-level verification, and the pure permission logic every downstream buyer surface checks. Output: Migration 062 (buyer_orgs, buyer_members, handle_new_user buyer branch, RLS + column-privilege lockdown), lib/buyers schema/permission/org modules, and a green permissions test.
</objective>
