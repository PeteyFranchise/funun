<objective>
Give artists the two surfaces the consent model depends on: a per-project pre-cleared terms editor (D-15 — artists pre-clear rather than approving every request) and a dedicated Deals sidebar room listing every license request across their projects with deal stages (D-15b).

Purpose: Pre-cleared terms are what let a matching request skip straight past admin negotiation (D-15a), and the Deals room is the artist's whole window into commercial demand. Buyer↔artist messaging is deliberately absent — beta communication is admin-mediated (D-14b).
Output: Licensing terms API + form mounted on the rights surface, the /deals room with stage-labelled request list, and the ArtistNav entry.
</objective>
