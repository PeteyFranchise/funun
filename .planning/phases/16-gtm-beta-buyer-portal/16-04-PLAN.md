# 16-04: Deal Room, Contract Locker & E-Sign Handoff

## Objective

Plan the internal deal workflow that turns a submitted license request into quote, legal review, contract artifact, signed document, and closed deal.

## Scope

- Define admin/founder deal-room UI.
- Link license requests to artist/project/track records and readiness state.
- Link license requests to Contract Locker documents.
- Decide e-sign provider path: SignWell-first versus Dropbox/DocuSign fallback.
- Preserve upload-signed-PDF as fallback while provider webhooks are added.
- Define artist consent checkpoint and legal review status.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Request stages map clearly to deal-room actions.
- Contract Locker is the signed-document destination.
- Signed state can only be earned by uploaded signed PDF or provider webhook.
- Legal/product decision for Funun as licensor/agent versus artist as licensor is recorded before final contract automation.
- Provider decision checkpoint is required before live e-sign implementation.

## Validation

- Smoke test request-to-contract-link path.
- Regression test signed-state guard.
- Manual legal review checklist for contract fields.
