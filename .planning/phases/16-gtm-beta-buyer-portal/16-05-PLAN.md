# 16-05: GTM Beta Metrics, Enablement & Rollout Gates

## Objective

Plan the operating layer around the buyer portal so early GTM learning is measurable, repeatable, and safe to hand to future collaborators or an AE.

## Scope

- Define beta metrics dashboard or report source.
- Create buyer FAQ outline.
- Create artist readiness onboarding checklist outline.
- Create internal platform walkthrough outline.
- Create founder/AE playbook outline.
- Define rollout gates for broader buyer outreach, paid acquisition, and AE hiring.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Metrics are tied to `license_requests` lifecycle data.
- AE hiring is gated behind 3-5 closed deals and a written playbook.
- Paid acquisition is deferred until request-to-quote and quote-to-close baselines exist.
- Artist relations hiring is tied to supply/readiness bottlenecks, not generic growth.
- Enablement artifacts are versioned in the repo or docs system.

## Validation

- Manually review metrics against the revenue assumptions in the GTM plan.
- Confirm every metric has an owner and source field.
- Confirm the playbook can explain the full flow to a new helper without founder-only context.
